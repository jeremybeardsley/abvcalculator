ABV Calculations

An estimate of ABV % is calculated by subtracting the final gravity from the original gravity, and then multiplying by 131. This method has been known to have a fault of up to .5%, but is useful for calculating percentages in Home Brewing.

Measuring Gravity

Initial gravity is measured using a Hydrometer, prior to the fermenting process. Final Gravity is measured after the fermenting process has been complete.
